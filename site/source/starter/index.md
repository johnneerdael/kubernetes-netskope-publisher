A linear, top-to-bottom walkthrough that takes you from zero Kubernetes
experience to your first Publisher showing **Online** in the Netskope
admin console. The recommended path uses a single-node **k3s** cluster
on a Linux VM. Estimated time: ~30 minutes.

5. [Configure your shell](/kubernetes-netskope-publisher/starter/05-configure-shell/)
